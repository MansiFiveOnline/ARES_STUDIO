Financial Dashboards for better data insights

Getting the right information on your dashboard is vital to a business's success. The more you can see about performance and other factors, the better you can react to them and move forward. Our smart accounting dashboard services ensure you get the data and info you need while minimizing clutter.

What Are Our Accounting Dashboard Services?

If you are a QSR Franchise owner, odds are you want to convert that deceptively complex, ever-important question: "How is my business doing" into a smart dashboard.
Built by our engineers and vetted by our accountants, our dashboards are purpose-built, near real-time, highly visual, zero-scroll, and interactive. And all of this fits easily on your phone!:

Why Does My Company Need a Dashboard?

For one, it saves you valuable time not having to track your data and perform analytics. Why sweat the small stuff – let our dashboards take care of it.
Secondly, visuals engage your reptilian brain. Why? Because 90% of information transmitted to the brain is visual; speed-wise, the brain processes visual information 60,000 times faster than text. For example, if you want to find the 2's below, which picture gets you there faster?

Finally, why take 8-10 clicks to navigate to the data you need, if you can see all your business data in one click?
