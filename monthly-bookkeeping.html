Customized Solutions and Accommodations

If the client only needs to outsource certain aspects of their bookkeeping (e.g. bank reconciliation or accounts receivable reconciliation) we can accommodate them with personalized solutions.

  • Third-Party Processors: Indevia can also work with third-party invoice processors like Bill.com or ZohoExpense. However, we strongly recommend that the client keep final control of disbursements.
  • Secure Data Collection: We collect the source data (e.g. bank statements, credit card statements, point of sale results, credit card processor, payroll vendor, suppliers, etc.) electronically wherever feasible.
  • Immediate Integration: Transitioning to a new vendor is always daunting. However, we have streamlined the transition process. The accounting starts two days after the agreement is signed.
